Disease Spread

As COVID-19 became a headline in the news in the United States, I thought it would be interesting to do a simple model showing how disease spreads, and how various factors could change the dynamics. I started by creating a model with 100 particles, where initially one particle was infected. I gave each particle an initial velocity, both in the x and y direction, and made a rule saying if an uninfected particle came within a specific distance to an infected particle, it would also become infected. A person is only contagious for a specified amount of time, so I set a defined length of time when the particle would be able to spread the disease, and after that point, the particle was marked as 'recovered', so it could not transmit the disease or receive the disease.
